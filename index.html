MonneCare

About The Project

MonneCare is a digital pharmacy platform concept designed to provide easy access to healthcare. Users can search for medicines, consult with pharmacists, and manage prescription reminders, all in one integrated, user-friendly, and secure platform.

Gallery

My Journey & Learnings

The main challenge in this project was designing an interface that felt trustworthy and simple for users who might be anxious about health matters. This taught me the importance of user empathy in UI/UX design. I learned to prioritize clarity over complexity, ensuring every feature served a clear purpose, which reinforced my belief in a human-centered design approach.

Eco-Hub

About The Project

Eco-Hub is an innovative platform that connects you with waste banks, providing incentives and creating a sustainable recycling ecosystem. Turn your trash into digital blessings with ease.

Gallery

My Journey & Learnings

For Eco-Hub, the goal was to motivate users towards a sustainable habit. The challenge was not just technical, but psychological. I explored how gamification and a clear rewards system could drive user engagement. This project was a lesson in interdisciplinary thinking, connecting software engineering with behavioral psychology to build a product that encourages positive change.
